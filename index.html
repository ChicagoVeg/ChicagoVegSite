ChicagoVeg

For Earth ~ For Animals ~ For You

A Chicagoland Vegetarian, Vegan, & Raw Foodist Community

We are a non-for-profit organization to support all vegans/vegetarians and educate the public about the advantages of a plant-based diet. Join us for delicious meals, share your favorite recipes, discuss living a healthy (vegetarian - vegan - raw food) lifestyle, support the green movement, conscious living, and make new friends! We regularly meet in the city and suburbs for dining, picnics, lectures, movies, visiting local expos, and other events.
