ChicagoVeg Choice

Have you ever thought what is the most popular vegetarian/vegan restaurant in Chicago area? Or, perhaps, how other vegetarians/vegans rate the local veg*n restaurants? CHICAGOVEG CHOICE was established to answer this question, as well as to help promoting 100% vegetarian/vegan restaurants in Chicagoland. The results are solely based on anonymous polls that offered to all ChicagoVeg members in the end of the each year.
